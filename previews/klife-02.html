How to Use This Book

Understanding the Levels:

  • A1: Speaker A Focus, Korean -> English
  • A2: Speaker A Focus, English -> Korean
  • B1: Speaker B Focus, Korean -> English
  • B2: Speaker B Focus, English -> Korean

Recommended Study Order: A1 -> B1 -> A2 -> B2

Audio Access

Free audio files available at: nowgokim.github.io/kdrama-korean
